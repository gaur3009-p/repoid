Our Services

Comprehensive Dermatological Care

From medical dermatology to advanced cosmetic procedures, we offer evidence-based treatments using state-of-the-art technology.

Medical Dermatology

Treatment for acne, eczema, psoriasis, fungal infections, vitiligo, and all skin disorders with personalized care protocols.

Cosmetic Procedures

Botox, fillers, thread lifts, and non-surgical facial rejuvenation performed with precision for natural-looking results.

Laser Treatments

Advanced laser therapy for hair removal, pigmentation, scar reduction, tattoo removal, and skin resurfacing.

Skin Cancer Screening

Comprehensive mole mapping, dermoscopy, and early detection programs for melanoma and non-melanoma skin cancers.

Hair Restoration

PRP therapy, hair transplants, and medical management of alopecia for both men and women.

Aesthetic Surgery

Scar revision, liposuction, blepharoplasty, and minimally invasive body contouring procedures.

About the Doctor

Dr. Mamta Bhura

Dermatologist & Cosmetic Surgeon

With over 27 years of dedicated practice in dermatology and cosmetic surgery, Dr. Mamta Bhura has established herself as one of Kanpur's most trusted skin specialists. Her approach combines clinical expertise with aesthetic sensibility.

Trained in advanced dermatological procedures and cosmetic techniques, she has treated over 50,000 patients ranging from complex medical skin conditions to advanced aesthetic transformations.
